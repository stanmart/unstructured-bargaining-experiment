We will now discuss how the group is formed at the end of the round.

Group formation

During the bargaining stage, each player can choose which proposal they accept. Once the round ends, the final outcome and the payoffs of this round are determined as follows:

  • Only if all players in a proposed group agree on the same proposal is that proposal successful.
  • Note, that players who are not included in a proposal do not have to agree to it.
The group is then successfully formed and its members' payoffs are then determined by the agreed-upon proposal. All other players get 0. If there is no such agreement, all three players get 0.

Note that because Player {{P1}} has to be included for a group to receive a budget, there will be one group at most.
