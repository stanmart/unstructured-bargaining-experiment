Payment at the end of the experiment

Your payment at the end of the experiment consists of the base payment of 10 CHF and your payoff from one of the non-trial rounds. The round from which your payoff is taken is randomly determined at the end of the experiment, and is not influenced by your decisions or outcomes.

If you have any questions, please raise your hand now and an experimenter will come to you.

Otherwise, please proceed to the trial round.
